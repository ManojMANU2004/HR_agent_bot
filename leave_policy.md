## Annual / Casual Leave
- 24 paid leave days per calendar year
- Leave can be applied through the HR portal
- Up to 8 unused leave days can be carried forward to the next calendar year

## Sick Leave
- 10 paid sick days per year
- Doctor certificate required if absent for more than 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave cannot be encashed

## Maternity & Paternity Leave
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act)
- Paternity Leave: 15 days to be availed within 6 months of child birth

## Emergency / Bereavement Leave
- Up to 5 days with manager approval

## Holiday Calendar
- 12 public holidays per year as per company calendar

## Additional Notes
- Unplanned absence must be notified to reporting manager before 10:00 AM
