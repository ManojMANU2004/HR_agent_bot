# Employee FAQ – Manoj Tech Solutions

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Login to HR portal → Leaves → Apply. Enter dates and reason. Manager approval needed.

Q: How many annual leave days do employees receive?
A: 24 paid leaves per year.

Q: How many sick leaves are available?
A: 10 sick leaves per year.

Q: Who do I contact for payroll issues?
A: payroll@manojtech.com

Q: What is the insurance coverage amount?
A: INR 5,00,000 under family floater plan.

Q: Where can I download payslips?
A: Payroll portal → Payslips → Download.

Q: What is the pay day?
A: Last working day of each month.
