# Manoj Tech Solutions - HR Handbook

Welcome to Manoj Tech Solutions Pvt Ltd. This handbook provides essential HR guidelines for all employees.

## Work Hours
- Standard hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Monday to Friday)
- Core hours required for meetings: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
- Breaks: 1-hour lunch break; 2 short breaks are allowed

## Remote Work
- Hybrid model — employees may use up to 3 remote work days per week
- Fully remote roles require pre-approval from the HR department

## Workplace Behavior
- Maintain professionalism, respect, and inclusivity at all times
- Dress code: Smart casual (unless client-facing work requires formal attire)

## Grievance Redressal
- Employees may raise issues via HR ticketing portal or by emailing hr@manojtech.com
- All grievances are treated confidentially and resolved within 7–14 working days

## Travel Policy Summary
- Business travel requires manager approval before booking
- Eligible expenses: travel tickets, accommodation, meals up to INR 1,500/day
- Receipts must be submitted within 10 days of travel completion
